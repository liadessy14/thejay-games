🍭 HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click a candy, then click an adjacent candy to swap them.
  2. Match 3 or more identical candies in a horizontal or vertical line.
  3. Matched candies will disappear and earn points.
  4. New candies will fall to fill empty spaces.
  5. Create combo chains to earn more points.
  6. Reach the target score to complete the level.
  7. Complete levels to gain extra time.
  8. The game ends when the timer reaches 0.

Good luck! Reach the highest level and beat your high score! 🚀🏆
